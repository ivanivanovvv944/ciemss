Custom in ear monitors are basically a fancy term for custom made earphones for musicians.

The have 3 things, very good sound isolation due to mimicking shape of your ear canal, comfort for a long period of time, and complete freedom so customize the sound anyway you want, adding acoustic filters and deciding which drivers you want.


This is how I pictured it in my head (pun intended).
